Frank Curry
Overview

Frank Curry was a seaman that was enlisted into the Royal Canadian Navy. He started at the age of 19 and served for about 5 years. His journal is the only one standing that includes a perspective of a Canadian seaman during the war. Scroll down to see sources

Frank Curry's
Diary

In this diary, he talks about his journey at the sea and highlights his daily tasks and chores aboard the ship. He wrote his diary from 1940 all the way to 1945 which was most of his service time in the Navy.
